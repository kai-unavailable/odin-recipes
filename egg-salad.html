Egg Salad for Sandwiches

Egg Salad's main ingredient is usually for breakfast, but now it is used for lunch.

Yum Yum delicioso!

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 cup maypnnaise
  • 1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • salt and pepper for taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool, peel and chop.
  2. Place the chopped eggs in a bowl, and stir in the mayonnaise, mustard and green onion. Season with salt, pepper and paprika. Stir and serve on your favorite bread or crackers.
